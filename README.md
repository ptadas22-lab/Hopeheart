# HopeHeart

**Find someone safe to talk to.**

HopeHeart is a gentle emotional-support app for people who feel lonely, anxious, unheard, or afraid to share. It helps users pause, check in with their mood, reflect privately with HopeBuddy, save personal memories, and reach safe community support when they are ready.

HopeHeart is **emotional support only**. It does not diagnose, treat, prescribe, provide dosage advice, make cure claims, or replace doctors, therapists, hospitals, emergency care, or crisis services.

## Core Idea

Many people have a quiet moment when they want to say, “I am not okay,” but they stop because they do not know who is safe.

HopeHeart is built for that quiet moment.

**Trust before advice. Emotion before solution. Support before diagnosis.**

---

## User Journey

1. Open HopeHeart
2. Login or continue as guest
3. Check in with mood
4. Receive a small comfort reminder
5. Save a memory or reflection
6. Talk with HopeBuddy only when ready
7. Discover safe community support if needed

HopeHeart does not push users to talk immediately. It first helps them pause, feel, and remember themselves.

## Safety Rules

HopeHeart does not allow:

- Diagnosis
- Prescriptions
- Dosage advice
- Cure claims
- Treatment instructions
- Fake medical promises
- Harmful or abusive conversations

HopeHeart provides emotional support only and does not replace professional medical care.

---

## Sustainability Model

HopeHeart keeps core emotional support free.

Potential revenue streams:

- Verified professional listings
- Premium user tools
- Institution partnerships
- Referral or booking fees for verified external services

The model is designed to support access without selling emotional vulnerability.
